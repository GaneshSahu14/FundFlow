Gourav Foundation Scholarship

West Bengal - National

Scholarship Details

The Gaurav Foundation will be providing a scholarship to students who are doing well in their studies and have recently completed their undergraduate courses in India. The scholarship will only be available to students with good academic records. The scholarship application form will be available on 31st October at the official website of the Gaurav foundation and the candidates are requested to fill up the application form from the official website by sending a demand draft for 1500 Rupees addressed to the concerned organization.

Award

The applicant will be provided with the following awards in this scholarship:-

  • Students enrolled in Indian colleges are allowed a maximum scholarship reward of INR 2,00,000 per year.
  • Students enrolled in colleges abroad are allowed a maximum scholarship reward of INR 10,00,000 per year.
Eligibility

The applicant must follow the following eligibility criteria to be eligible for this scholarship:-

  • The scholarship is awarded to highly meritorious Indian students who have completed their undergraduate studies in India with good academic records to pursue their postgraduate studies in and outside of India.
  • The applicants must be Indian Nationals who are regular residents of India.
  • Applicants should not hold any other country’s passport.
  • Applicants should have a minimum of 60% in their class 10th and class 12th examinations.
  • He/ She should be below 35 years of age.
  • Applicants should be a graduate of a recognized university in India.
  • Students waiting for confirmation of admission to universities are also eligible to apply.
Documents Needed

Family Income (Varies from Applicant to Applicant)
Previous Educational History with Minimum 60% Marks
Documents Listed on the Main Form
Current / Proposed College & Fees Structure
3 Academic References (Teaching Staff)
Active / Valid Annual Member or Special Applicant of Gaurav Foundation
Filled Application Form
Verification & Other Procedures

Provider
Gaurav Foundation
How To Apply

Students can apply online and offline for the Gaurav Foundation scholarship. The detailed application process for both are listed below:

Online Process

  • The candidates must pay INR 1500 through an online payment method and then proceed with the application form which can be downloaded by clicking here.
  • The application form can be downloaded and filled carefully.
  • Candidates must attach photocopies of the required documents along with the application form and mail it to Gaurav Foundation, G-107, Vidyasagar Oswal Garden, 210-212, Cochrane Basin Road, Korukkupet, Chennai - 600021.

Offline Process

  • Candidate can follow the same procedure as mentioned for online application but instead of online payment, they can send a demand draft of INR 1500 to the above-mentioned address.

General Information

  • The application form cannot be refunded.
  • Photocopy of application form is not allowed.
