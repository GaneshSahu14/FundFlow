Post-Matric (Other Than Intermediate) Scholarship for OBC

Uttar Pradesh - National

Scholarship Details

Post-Matric (Other Than Intermediate) Scholarship for OBC, Uttar Pradesh 2023-24 is an initiative of the Backward Classes Welfare Department, Government of Uttar Pradesh, for students pursuing studies at the graduation or higher level. The selected students will receive a tuition fee reimbursement of up to ₹50,000 or the actual fees paid, whichever is less.

Award

The selected students will receive a reimbursement of the annual compulsory non-refundable fees, as approved by the competent authority for the respective courses, which will be provided to selected students in the following categories:

GroupCourse levelReimbursement Limit
1Technical courses (graduate/postgraduate)Up to ₹50,000 or actual fees paid, whichever is less
2Non-technical/vocational courses (postgraduate)Up to ₹30,000 or actual fees paid, whichever is less
3Undergraduate non-technical/vocationalUp to ₹20,000 or actual fees paid, whichever is less
4Technical/professional courses at the intermediate and equivalent levelUp to ₹10,000 or actual fees paid, whichever is less

 

Eligibility
  • be an Indian citizen
  • be domiciled in Uttar Pradesh
  • belong to the Other Backward Classes (OBC) category
  • be studying graduation, postgraduation, or higher level degree at a recognised institution/university
  • have an annual parent(s)/guardian income of less than ₹2,00,000 per annum from all sources

  • Note:- Students pursuing below courses are ineligible for the scholarship:

    • Aircraft Maintenance Engineer Course
    • Private Pilot License course
    • Training course of Sainik High School, Dehradun
    • All India and state-level pre-examination training
    • Correspondence courses and distance 
    • Course for Commercial Pilot License (CPL)
    • In courses conducted by private sector training institutes where certificates/marks are not awarded by any university, affiliating board, or authorised department, and the examination is not conducted by an authorised agency at a standardized level, i.e., in courses that lack uniform control and standardization
Documents Needed
  • Residential proof including Electricity bill, Ration card, Voter ID, Aadhaar card, PAN, or Driving license
  • Caste certificate
  • A passport-size photograph
  • Annual family income certificate 
  • Marksheets/certificates of qualifying examinations
  • Current year fee receipt/admission letter
  • Domicile certificate
  • Birth certificate
  • Student ID proof
  • Bank passbook of the student
Provider
Backward Classes Welfare Department, Government of Uttar Pradesh
How To Apply

Eligible students can apply for the scholarship through the following steps:-
Step 1: Click on the 'Apply Now' link below. 
Step 2: Click on the 'Register' button and fill in the required registration details. (Note:- If already registered, log in using Gmail/Mobile number/Email ID).
Step 3: Navigate to the 'Students' tab on the top left-hand of the dashboard and click on the 'Registration' button (Note:- If the applicant is registered, kindly click on the 'Fresh Login' button and choose the scholarship).
Step 4: Click on the ' Postmatric Other Than Intermediate (Fresh)' link, under the 'For OBC Category' section.
Step 5: Fill in all required details to 'Register' and click on the Generate OTP button.  
Step 6: After successful registration, log in using valid credentials.
Step 7: Fill in the required details, upload the relevant documents, and click on the 'Submit' button.
